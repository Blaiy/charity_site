Our Story

Okoa Kaka na Dada, Non-Profit Organization

Okoa Kaka na Dada was born out of a profound desire to make a difference in our community. Founded by a group of passionate students from Lugulu High School, we recognized the pressing needs around us and decided to take action. During our holidays, we would visit children’s homes, bringing donations and volunteering our time. These experiences opened our eyes to the vast challenges faced by many and inspired us to create a more structured and impactful approach. Thus, Okoa Kaka na Dada was established, rooted in the values of compassion, community, and commitment to change.

Our Mission

At Okoa Kaka na Dada, our mission is to uplift and empower vulnerable communities in Kenya by providing essential resources and opportunities. We strive to create a sustainable impact through our core initiatives:

  • Basic Needs
  • Empowerment and Support

Our Causes

Children Education

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. We support children's education by providing school supplies, funding scholarships, and creating after-school programs. Our aim is to give every child the opportunity to learn, grow, and achieve their full potential.

Poverty Development

We believe in empowering communities to rise out of poverty. Our poverty development programs focus on providing skills training, micro-loans, and resources to start small businesses. We work with individuals and families to develop sustainable solutions that improve their livelihoods and create lasting change.

Raised: Ksh276,000

Goal: Ksh600,000

Supply drinking water

Access to clean and safe drinking water is a fundamental human right. At Okoa Kaka na Dada, we work tirelessly to provide clean water to communities in need. We install water purification systems, repair wells, and educate communities on safe water practices. Our goal is to ensure that every person has access to this basic necessity.
